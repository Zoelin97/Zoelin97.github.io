Paloma Recipie

Step 1

  • PREPARE ALL INGREDIENTS
  • 50ml Tequila
  • 50ml freshly squeezed pink grapefruit juice
  • 15ml freshly squeezed lime
  • 15ml agave nectar
  • Top up with Grapefruit soda water
>

Step 2

Shake the first 4 ingredients together in cocktail shaker with ice.

Step 3

Then pour into a glass filled with large ice cubes and top with grapefruit soda.

Step 4

Garnish with a slice of grapefruit or lime.
