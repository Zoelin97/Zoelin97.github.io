Jungle Bird Recipie

Step 1

  • PREPARE ALL INGREDIENTS
  • 1 1⁄2 oz Dark rum
  • 3⁄4 oz Campari
  • 1⁄2 oz Demerara syrup
  • 1 1⁄2 oz Fresh pineapple juice
  • 1⁄2 oz Fresh lime juice
>

Step 2

AddFill a mixing glass three-quarters with ice, and add all of the ingredients.

Step 3

Shake until chilled for about 30 seconds.

Step 4

Strain into a rocks glass over ice cubes or one large piece of ice, and add the garnish.
